Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

We respect your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

1 What we collect

We collect your email address and any message you send us through the contact or waitlist form. We also collect basic analytics data such as page views and device type to improve the site.

2 How we use it

We use your email to send you updates about Founder's Diary, launch announcements, and early access invites.

shield

We do not sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing — ever.

3 Data storage

Your data is stored securely with our email and hosting providers. We only keep it as long as needed to contact you about Founder's Diary.

4 Your choices

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link at the bottom of any email we send. To request deletion of your data, email us at support@foundersdiary.eu.org.

5 Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies for basic analytics. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6 Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@foundersdiary.eu.org.
